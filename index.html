Open all year round! All skill levels welcome!

We are a small family run riding establishment. We offer some of the most beautiful and picturesque rides around Cheshire. Exploring the fantastic Beeston and Peckforton castle grounds and the Bickerton Hills, venturing onto the the sandstone trail with all their splendid views. We ride along the Bishop Bennetts way and many more of the famous Cheshire bridal paths.

We offer a 1,2 or 3 hour rides daily or even the mini break, two days of riding plus an overnight stay in our private, self-catering accommodation with a private garden, it is the perfect way too get away from it all.

Ideal for walkers too, as it is ideally located close to many great walks and trails on foot or why not even bring your bike? We are closely situated to some wonderful attractions, such as the famous Cheshire Ice-cream farm, the candle workshop and the Pheasant Inn, making it an ideal location for a getaway.

Horse Riding Adventures start from as little as £15/hour per person.

Weekend Breaks from £150/hour per person.
